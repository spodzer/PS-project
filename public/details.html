Def Jam: Fight for NY

Def Jam: Fight for NY is a hip hop-inspired fighting game developed by Aki Corporation and published by Electronic Arts for the PlayStation 2. It was released as the sequel to the popular Def Jam: Vendetta. The game features a roster of popular hip-hop artists, and players can battle it out in various fighting styles. It is highly praised for its unique blend of hip hop culture and intense brawling action.

This game is rare and sought after by collectors due to its unique crossover of music and fighting genres, and its limited availability in certain regions.

Release: 2004

Price: $279.99
